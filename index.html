About the Author

Eleanor May Carter (Also known as Ipsywitch) is a digital author and fanfic writer from Newark-on-Trent, in the midlands of England, Europe. Born 2nd of August, 1992, Eleanor has been writing fanfics, stories that take place in a fictional world of popular media, since she graduated high school in July 2010. Along with a creative imagination, Eleanor also shows a good understanding on computers and cyberspace, using it to post her works and upcoming projects on multiple media. Raised by a strong and caring single mother, Eleanor has been encouraged to continue writing and work on projects that show her creativity and fulfill her dreams as a possible accomplished author.

Meet the Characters

Mac the Robot

Mac is one of the main protagonists of the sci-fi, survival story: Year 2250, The Frozen World. Mac is a female robot that awoken within an abandoned science facility, along with five other robots and has placed the task upon herself to lead the group through the hostile world and find answers to their questions. She goes through a spiritual journey to discover who she really was before deactivating and why she and the other robots are left alive.
Link: Robot Girl Link

Inque the Alien

Inque is one of the three main protagonists of a sci-fi and teen story: This Strange Home. Inque is an extra-terrestrial born on the planet Neptune but was sent away and arrived on Earth 50 years from the present day. 50 years later, Inque finds herself rescued and brought home by a small group of teenagers from Dallas, Texas. As Inque learns the secret behind her visit to Earth, the teens learn about her species and try to find a way to send the young extra-terrestrial back to her home planet, as well as keep her hidden from the government and FBI trying to track her down and bring her back to their research center.
Link: Octopus Girl Link

Alba the Kraken

Alba Sakana is one of the main characters of a fanfiction story based on the Teenage Kraken movie. She is Ruby Gillman's cousin on her mother's side, so that makes her a Royal Giant Kraken. Being the same age as Ruby, Alba attends the same school, but instead of being a mathlete, Alba is a nerd/geek that enjoys science and comic books. Alongside her cousin, Alba learns the destiny of her heritage and helps Ruby find the right path to her destiny to defeat the Mermaid Queen and stop hiding her true nature.
